Fuck Farhi

London's biggest slumlord is hoarding our city. It's time to fight back.

December 27 Was a Huge Success

Our protest at Richmond & Dufferin brought Londoners together to stand up against property hoarding and demand accountability. The energy was incredible and the message was clear: we won't accept empty buildings while people sleep outside.

We're now planning next steps for the campaign. If you'd like to get involved, join our mailing list to stay in the loop on upcoming actions and meetings.

While Shmuel Farhi sits on thousands of empty properties waiting for land values to rise, Londoners are sleeping on the streets. Property hoarding isn't just bad business — it's a weapon against our community.

Use It or Lose It

We demand an end to property hoarding. Vacant buildings must be taxed heavily, occupied within mandatory timelines, or expropriated for social housing.

"Empty buildings while people sleep outside is a policy choice. We can make a different choice."

Our Demands:

  • Aggressive vacant property taxes that escalate yearly
  • Mandatory occupancy timelines with real enforcement
  • Expropriation of chronically vacant properties for social housing
  • Transparency on all property holdings by major landlords
