May Day 2026

The London May Day Committee is planning the biggest International Workers' Day celebration London has ever seen. May 1st falls on a Friday this year, and we're building a full weekend of events - rallies, workshops, music, art, education, and solidarity actions.

The committee is organized on the basis of contingents. Each endorsing organization is encouraged to mobilize a contingent with their own leaflet and slogans. We're seeking endorsements from unions, community groups, and socialist organizations across London.

The London May Day Committee is based on the independence of the working class and oppressed peoples from the Canadian State. We coordinate our own team of marshalls to ensure the safety of all rally and march participants.

Want to get involved? Whether you want to help plan, bring a contingent from your organization, or just show up and march - join our mailing list to stay in touch and take part in the May Day Committee.
