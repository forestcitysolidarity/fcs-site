Forest City Solidarity

Forest City Solidarity is a community-based mutual aid and mutual defense organization in London, Ontario offering active solidarity to individuals and groups under attack.

Join the Fight Events

What We Do

Direct Action

We show up for each other — at eviction defences, picket lines, and anywhere our neighbours need backup.

Resource Sharing

From tool libraries to grocery runs, we build the infrastructure of mutual aid.

Rapid Response

When someone faces a crisis, we mobilize. No one fights alone.

Popular Education

We learn together about tenants' rights, labour organizing, and building power from below.

Our Values

  • Anti-colonial
  • Pro-feminist
  • Anti-racist
  • 2SLGBTQI+-positive
